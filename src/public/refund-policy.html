Refund Policy

Last Updated: November 17, 2024

7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all monthly and lifetime subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with ClickBlock for any reason, you can request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase.

Trial Period

The $1 trial fee is non-refundable once the trial period begins. You can cancel anytime during the trial to avoid being charged for the full subscription.

Monthly Subscriptions

Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. Refunds are available within the first 7 days of your initial subscription or renewal. After 7 days, no refund will be issued, but you will retain access until the end of your billing period.

Lifetime Plans

Lifetime plans are eligible for a full refund within 7 days of purchase. After 7 days, lifetime plans are non-refundable.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team at support@clickblock.co with:

  • Your account email
  • Order/Transaction ID
  • Reason for refund (optional but appreciated for improving our service)

Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days.

Exceptions

Refunds may be denied if:

  • Your account has been suspended or terminated for violations of our Terms of Service
  • You have submitted fraudulent or abusive refund requests in the past
  • The request is made after the 7-day refund window

Contact Us

If you have questions about our refund policy, please email us at support@clickblock.co.
